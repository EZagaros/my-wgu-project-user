Attractions

Pristine Beaches

Taniti offers both sandy and rocky beaches encircling Yellow Leaf Bay. Enjoy sunbathing on white sands, snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, or exploring tide pools along our scenic coastline.

Best beaches near Taniti City • Free access • Snorkel gear rentals available

Lush Rainforests

Explore our tropical rainforests through guided hikes or exhilarating zip-line tours. Discover exotic wildlife, cascading waterfalls, and breathtaking views from canopy platforms.

Zip-line tours daily • Hiking trails for all levels • Guided tours $35-75

Active Volcano

Visit Taniti's small but active volcano in the mountainous interior. Safe viewing areas allow you to witness steam vents and learn about the island's dramatic geology.

Bus tours available • Best views at sunset • Volcanic rock souvenirs

Taniti History Museum

Learn about the island's indigenous culture and colonial history through artifacts, photographs, and interactive exhibits. The museum showcases Taniti's transition from fishing village to tourist destination.

Open Tuesday-Sunday • $5 admission • English tours at 11am & 2pm

Merriton Landing

This rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay features art galleries, a microbrewery, dance club, movie theater, arcade, bowling alley, and helicopter tour operators.

Nightlife hub • Family-friendly by day • New golf course opening soon

Water Adventures

Enjoy chartered fishing tours, snorkeling in coral reefs, or boat tours around the island. Our small but safe harbor offers easy access to marine excursions.

Equipment rentals available • Half-day tours from $50 • Best spots near the harbor

Coming Soon: Golf Course

A new nine-hole golf course is under construction and will be operational next year, offering players stunning ocean views amidst tropical scenery.

Opening 2026 • Pro shop • Club rentals available
