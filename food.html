Food & Dining

Restaurants

Local Tanitian Cuisine

Five restaurants specializing in fresh local fish served with coconut rice and tropical fruits. Try our signature grilled mahi-mahi or banana-leaf-wrapped seafood dishes.

Best for: Authentic island flavors • Average meal: $12-20

American-Style Meals

Three restaurants offering familiar comfort food including burgers, steaks, and classic American dishes. Perfect for travelers craving a taste of home.

Best for: Family dining • Average meal: $15-25

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two restaurants featuring a mix of Thai, Japanese, and Chinese dishes. Enjoy sushi, stir-fries, curries, and other Asian favorites with island ingredients.

Best for: Variety • Average meal: $18-30

Grocery Options

Supermarkets

Two full-service supermarkets offering everything from fresh produce to local specialties. Stock up for your stay or grab picnic supplies.

Open daily 8am-9pm • Located in Taniti City

24-Hour Convenience Store

One always-open store for late-night snacks, drinks, and basic supplies. Perfect for emergencies or midnight cravings.

Open 24/7 • Near Merriton Landing

Dining Tips & Policies

  • Alcohol Laws: Not served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • Drinking Age: 18 years old (enforcement is relaxed)
  • Payment: Most accept U.S. dollars, euros, yen, and major credit cards
  • Holiday Closures: Some restaurants close during national holidays
  • Local Specialties: Try coconut rice pudding and tropical fruit platters
