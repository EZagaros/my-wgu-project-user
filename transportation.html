Getting to Taniti

By Air

Most visitors fly into Taniti's small airport, which currently accommodates jets and propeller planes. Exciting expansion plans will soon allow larger aircraft to land, making your journey even more convenient.

Airport Code: TNT | Airlines: Island Air, Pacific Connect, Taniti Express

By Sea

Experience the romance of ocean travel as our small cruise ship docks weekly at picturesque Yellow Leaf Bay. Perfect for travelers who want to combine their island stay with a memorable sea journey.

Cruise Line: Island Odyssey Cruises | Docking Day: Every Saturday

Getting Around Taniti

Public Transportation

Our efficient transit system makes exploring easy. Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily, while private buses connect you to more remote island locations.

Fares: $2 per ride or $10 unlimited day pass | Taxi Stands: Available at all major hotels and attractions

Bike & Walking

Taniti City's flat terrain makes it perfect for walking and cycling. Rent bikes by the hour or day from numerous locations near popular tourist areas.

Safety Note: Helmets are required by law and provided with all rentals | Walking Tours: Available daily at 9am and 2pm

Car Rentals

For maximum flexibility, rent a car from our airport location or downtown Taniti City. Drive on well-maintained roads and explore at your own pace.

Requirements: Valid driver's license | Note: Driving is on the right side of the road
